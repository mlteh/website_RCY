RED CROSS YOUTH

National University of Singapore Chapter

Red Cross Youth - National University of Singapore (RCY - NUS) Chapter started our humble origins in 1994, and has since expanded to a range of 8 different projects. Our projects range across various aspects such as humanitarian aid and community service. Members of the RCY will be affiliated to the Singapore Red Cross, one of the largest international humanitarian movement in the world. Register with us as a member HERE.

Vision of Singapore Red Cross

To realise Singapore Red Cross as a leading and distinctive humanitarian organisation that brings people and institutions together in aid of the vulnerable.

Mission of Singapore Red Cross

Singapore Red Cross is dedicated to relieving human suffering, protecting human lives and dignity and responding to emergencies.
